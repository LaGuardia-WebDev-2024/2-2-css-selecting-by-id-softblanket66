All about Fox

BasicInfo

Fox is awesome because they have a orange and white long fur. Foxes are small-to-medium-sized omnivores mammals belonging to several genera of the family Canidae. They have a flattened skull; upright, triangular ears; a pointed, slightly upturned snout; and a long, bushy tail.
